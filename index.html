Plan Phases

JUNE 2026 — FEBRUARY 2027

NOW — 30 JUNE 2026
Foundation Phase
Return to Malta. Establish habits — IF 16:8, MyFitnessPal tracking, supplement stack, PPL x2. Start running 3x/week at easy pace. Begin weight loss from 84kg.
1 JULY — 31 AUGUST 2026
Base Building Phase
PwC starts. Train around work schedule. Run mileage builds to 25km/week. Target weight 79–80kg by end of August. Book GP for blood recheck and core assessment.
SEPTEMBER — OCTOBER 2026
Half Marathon Specific Block
Introduce tempo runs. Long run builds to 17–19km. Target weight 75–76kg. Weekly mileage peaks at 33–36km. Tune up race in September if available.
1 NOVEMBER 2026
Taper Begins
Reduce volume 30%. Maintain intensity. Prioritise sleep and nutrition. Hit 75kg target. Final long run 12km two weeks before race.
15 NOVEMBER 2026
🏅 Żurrieq Half Marathon
Target: 1:45:00. Race registration open now at maltamarathon.com
NOVEMBER — DECEMBER 2026
Recovery + Marathon Block Begins
2 weeks recovery post half. Begin marathon specific training. Long runs build toward 32–34km peak. Maintain weight at 75kg.
JANUARY 2027
Marathon Peak Training
Highest mileage weeks. Long run 32–34km. Reduce gym volume to 3x per week. Prioritise sleep and nutrition above all else.
FEBRUARY 2027
Marathon Taper
Final 3 weeks — reduce volume progressively. Stay consistent, trust the training. Race week — rest Thursday and Friday.
28 FEBRUARY 2027
🏅 Malta Marathon — Mdina to Sliema
Goal: Finish strong. Downhill certified course, 195m net drop. Projected time 3:45–3:50 based on half marathon fitness.
